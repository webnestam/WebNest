Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with WebNest

How long does it take to build a website?
Most projects are completed within 2-4 weeks, depending on complexity and content availability. Simple sites can be ready in as little as 1-2 weeks, while more complex projects with custom features may take 4-6 weeks. Rush delivery is available for an additional fee.
What's included in the monthly maintenance plan?
The $300/month plan includes unlimited content updates, design edits, security monitoring, performance optimization, and priority support. You can request changes anytime via email or phone, and I'll implement them quickly‚Äîusually within 24-48 hours for standard updates.
Do I own the website after it's built?
Yes! You own all the code and content. With the one-time build option, you receive full access to everything immediately. With the maintenance plan, you maintain ownership throughout, and can cancel anytime to take full control without any restrictions.
Can you help with domain and hosting?
Absolutely! I can guide you through purchasing a custom domain and setting up hosting. Domain costs are typically $15-30/year, and hosting ranges from $5-20/month depending on your needs. I'll help you choose the best options and handle all technical setup.
What if I need additional features later?
Additional features and pages can be added anytime. If you're on the maintenance plan, small additions are often included. Larger features like e-commerce, booking systems, or custom integrations are quoted separately based on complexity.
What happens if I want to cancel the maintenance plan?
You can cancel anytime with 30 days notice. There are no long-term contracts or cancellation fees. You'll retain full ownership of your website and all the code, and I'll help ensure a smooth transition.
